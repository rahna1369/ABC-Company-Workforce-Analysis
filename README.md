# ABC-Company-Workforce-Analysis
A data analysis project using Python and Pandas to analyze employee distribution, demographics, and salary trends for ABC Company.

### Data Story
#### 1. **Team Distribution**
* **​The Findings:** The New Orleans Pelicans and Memphis Grizzlies are the largest teams by headcount.
* * **​The Insight:** These teams likely serve as the primary operational hubs for ABC Company.
#### ​2. **Position Distribution**
​* **The Findings:** Shooting Guards (SG) lead the count at 22.3%, while Centers (C) are the smallest group at 17.2%.
* **​The Insight:** The workforce is well-balanced across all five key positions.
#### ​3. **Age Demographics**
* **​The Findings:** The predominant age group is 25–29, with the second-largest being 20–24.
* * **​The Insight:** The company is powered by a high-energy, early-to-mid-career demographic.
#### ​4. **Financial Expenditure** 
* **The Findings:** The Cleveland Cavaliers represent the highest total salary expenditure.
* ​**The Insight:** Despite being the smallest group, the Center (C) position commands the highest total salary expenditure, suggesting these are the highest-valued                       roles.
#### 5. **Age and Salary Correlation**
* **​The Findings:** Our analysis yielded a correlation coefficient of 0.21.
* **​The Insight:** This weak positive correlation indicates that salary is driven more by position and merit than by seniority alone.
